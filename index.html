Let's Check Whether your birthday is palindrome or not.

A palindrome is a word, number, phrase or other sequence of characters which reads the same backward as forward.

For eg. The word madam or the number 12321 reads same when read backward & forward.

Enter you DOB and we'll let you know whether your birthday is palindrome.

To increase your chances of winning this app checks your DOB in 3 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yy
